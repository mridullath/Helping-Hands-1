Our Mission

WHAT

Our mission is to bridge communication gaps by converting sign language into text or speech, promoting inclusivity, accessibility, and seamless interaction between Deaf and hearing communities

HOW

We are creating an intuitive sign language interpretation website, employing Figma for design, the Canvas API for live video input, CNN models for accurate translation, and SQL for data management, to deliver a powerful and user-friendly tool for the community.

WHO

Our model can be used by all, may it be for learning sign language or for instant translation for understanding the speaker.

WHY

The idea of sign language interpreters sprouts from the need for effective communication between deaf/mute and hearing individuals promoting accessibility, inclusion, and equal opportunities in various settings.
